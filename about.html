Who Am I?

About Me

I spent the first 12 years of my working life as a Health Care Support Worker in a Forensic Psychiatric Unit with the NHS. However, 2 years ago I decided to go back to university and enrolled in an Arts and Humanities degree focusing on Art History and Music with the Open University.

I have always been fascinated by coding but never really thought I could do it. Just before the Covid Crisis I found SheCodes who run online Coding Workshops for women. I started their 1st course and I'm now on the 3rd. I have learnt so much in such a short space of time and they make a sometimes challenging subject fun and easy to understand! Also during the Covid Crisis I started painting (more on that in 'My Interests').

Now I want to put into practice all that I have learnt with art and coding to design and develop websites, particularly for creative people and businesses like me!
